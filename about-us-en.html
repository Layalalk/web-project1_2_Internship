About Us

A student internship platform developed by a team of University of Jeddah students to make it easier to find co-op and summer internship opportunities in Jeddah, and to provide a clearer and more organized search and recommendation experience.

📨 Our Mission

Empower internship seekers to find suitable opportunities in Jeddah through a platform that brings listings together and presents them clearly to support better career decisions.

We aim to reduce the time and effort spent searching by organizing content and providing tools to compare and filter opportunities using clear criteria.

💡 Our Vision

To become a trusted reference for discovering high-quality internship opportunities in Jeddah that improve job-market readiness and support experience building.

We focus on a simple user experience and structured information, with future improvements toward smart recommendations to better match opportunities with user skills and interests.
